Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Introduction

BoxCost Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our corrugated box costing and quotation management application.

Service Provider: DCore Systems LLP, a limited liability partnership registered in India.

Key Principle: We only collect and use data that is necessary to provide our core service. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for authentication and account recovery
  • Name: For personalization and identification
  • Password: Stored in encrypted (hashed) form using industry-standard bcrypt algorithm

2.2 Business Profile Information

To enable quotation generation, you provide:

  • Company/Business name
  • Business address
  • GST number (optional, for tax-compliant quotations)
  • Phone number
  • Company logo (optional)
  • Bank account details (optional, for payment instructions on quotations)

2.3 Google Account Data (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google or enable Gmail integration, we access:

  • Email address: For authentication and as sender address for quotations
  • Profile information: Name and profile picture (if available)
  • Gmail send permission (gmail.send scope): Only if you enable email quotation sending. We do NOT read, access, or store your existing emails. We only send new emails when you explicitly click "Send Quotation".

2.4 Customer & Business Data

Data you enter to manage your business:

  • Customer records (names, addresses, contact details, GST numbers)
  • Product specifications (box dimensions, paper types, pricing)
  • Quotation history (dates, items, prices, terms)
  • Price master data (material costs, labor rates)

2.5 Usage Data

Automatically collected technical information:

  • Login timestamps and session duration
  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited within the application
  • Error logs and performance metrics

We DO NOT collect: Financial transaction data (we are not a payment processor), biometric data, location tracking data, or data from third-party sources.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure:

  • Database hosted on secure PostgreSQL servers
  • Application hosted on reliable cloud platforms
  • Data centers located in compliance with Indian data protection regulations

4.2 Security Measures

Technical Safeguards:

  • All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption (minimum TLS 1.2)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt (never stored in plain text)
  • Database access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security updates and vulnerability patching
  • Session management with secure, httpOnly cookies
  • Protection against SQL injection, XSS, and CSRF attacks
  • Regular automated backups with encryption at rest

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: After 3 years of inactivity, we may send a notice before deletion
  • Deleted accounts: Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Legal obligations: Some data may be retained longer if required by Indian tax or business laws (e.g., GST records for 6 years)

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

BoxCost Pro does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Period.

5.2 Limited Disclosure Scenarios

We may share your data only in these specific circumstances:

Service Providers (Data Processors):

  • Cloud hosting providers: To store and serve the application
  • Email service (Gmail API): Only if you authorize email sending (Google acts as processor)
  • Payment gateway (if implemented): For processing subscription payments

All service providers are bound by strict data protection agreements and process data only on our instructions.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation (e.g., tax authorities, law enforcement with valid warrant).

Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of DCore Systems LLP, your data may be transferred to the successor entity (you will be notified in advance).

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the application at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information via Settings page
  • Export: Download your quotations and customer data in PDF/Excel format
  • Deletion: Request complete account deletion (contact support@paperboxerp.com)
  • Withdraw consent: Revoke Google account permissions via Google Account settings
  • Opt-out: Disable email notifications in Settings (you cannot opt-out of essential service emails)

6.2 Google Account Permissions

If you connected your Google account:

  • You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions
  • Revoking access will disable email sending but won't delete your BoxCost Pro account
  • You can re-authorize at any time

6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To request data deletion, export, or assistance with your rights, email us at support@paperboxerp.com with subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 15 business days.

8. Data Protection for Indian Users

As an India-based service, we comply with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 and its amendments
  • Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (once implemented)

Sensitive Personal Data: We handle GST numbers, bank details, and business information as sensitive data with enhanced security measures.

Cross-border transfers: If data is stored outside India, we ensure adequate safeguards through contractual clauses and encryption.

9. Children's Privacy

BoxCost Pro is a business application not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.
