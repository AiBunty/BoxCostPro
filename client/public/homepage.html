BoxCost Pro

Corrugated box costing calculator and quotation management system for packaging manufacturers

Developed and operated by DCore Systems LLP

What Does BoxCost Pro Do?

BoxCost Pro is a specialized business application designed for corrugated box and packaging manufacturers in India. The application performs the following core functions:

  1. Box Costing Calculation: Calculates accurate box pricing based on paper specifications (BF/Bursting Factor, GSM/grams per square meter), box dimensions (length, width, height), number of paper layers (3-ply, 5-ply, 7-ply), and wastage percentages.
  2. GST-Compliant Quotation Generation: Creates professional quotations with company letterhead, product details, pricing, GST calculations (5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%), and terms & conditions that comply with Indian tax regulations.
  3. Master Data Management: Maintains records of:
    • Party/Customer master (company names, GST numbers, shipping addresses, contact details)
    • Product master (box specifications, standard sizes, pricing templates)
    • Price master (rate cards, material costs, labor charges)
  4. Quote Distribution: Sends quotations directly to customers via:
    • Email (using user's Gmail account if authorized)
    • WhatsApp (via integration)
  5. Reporting & Analytics: Provides historical data on quotations sent, profit margin analysis, pricing trends, and customer inquiry tracking.

Target Users: Small to medium-sized corrugated box manufacturing units, packaging companies, and paper product businesses operating in India.

Data We Access & Why

BoxCost Pro requests access to the following user data. Below is a complete explanation of what data is collected and how it is used:

  • Email Address (via Google OAuth):
    • Purpose: Used for user authentication, login verification, and as the sender address when sending quotations via email.
    • How it's used: Your email is displayed in the application header and used as the "From" address when you send quotes to customers.
  • Business Profile Information:
    • Data collected: Company name, business address, GST number (if applicable), phone number, and company logo (optional).
    • Purpose: This information is required to generate professional, legally compliant quotations. Your business details appear on every quotation you create.
    • How it's stored: Stored securely in our database and only accessible by your account.
  • Google Account Access (Optional - Gmail API):
    • Scope requested: Send email on your behalf (gmail.send scope)
    • Purpose: If you choose to enable email sending, BoxCost Pro will send quotation emails directly from your Gmail account to your customers.
    • Control: This is entirely optional. You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account settings.
    • What we do NOT access: We do not read, store, or access any of your existing emails. We only send new emails when you explicitly click "Send Quotation".
  • Customer & Quotation Data:
    • Data you enter: Customer names, addresses, product specifications, pricing, and quotation history.
    • Purpose: To maintain your business records, generate quotes, and provide reporting.
    • Ownership: This data belongs to you. You can export or delete it at any time.

How BoxCost Pro Works

  1. Sign Up: Create an account using your Google account (OAuth login) or email/password.
  2. Set Up Business Profile: Enter your company details, GST number, and upload your logo.
  3. Add Customers: Create customer records with contact details and shipping addresses.
  4. Configure Products: Define standard box sizes, paper specifications, and pricing templates.
  5. Generate Quotations: Select customer, enter box specifications, calculate pricing, and create a quotation.
  6. Send & Track: Send quotations via email or WhatsApp, and track responses in the system.
